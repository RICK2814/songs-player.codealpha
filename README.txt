This folder is intended to hold your audio files for the music player.

Please add your mp3 files here with the following names:
- song1.mp3
- song2.mp3
- song3.mp3

Alternatively, you can replace the song URLs in script.js with URLs to online audio files for testing.

Example free sample audio URLs you can use:
- https://www.soundhelix.com/examples/mp3/SoundHelix-Song-1.mp3
- https://www.soundhelix.com/examples/mp3/SoundHelix-Song-2.mp3
- https://www.soundhelix.com/examples/mp3/SoundHelix-Song-3.mp3

Replace the 'src' property in the songs array in script.js accordingly.
